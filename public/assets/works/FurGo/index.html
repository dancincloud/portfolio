DESIGN DESCRIPTION
The chosen structure is minimal and classic while able to rotate as needed. Short and fat body makes it stable to sit on a surface with potential pets’ crazy motion. 75° and 180° captures entire view of room when placing by the wall, allowing users to watch pets wherever they jump to.

Design Considerations
Waterproof
Seamless and secure assembly allow pet to run in a high speed and play in the puddle, water fountain, or anywhere.

Night light
Easy to spot during night walk, can be turned on through APP. Serve as a backup method with sound.

Play sound
Easy to find pets during the day or somewhere lights prevail. Also serve as a backup method with night light.

Dark gray
Dirt resistant color for pets requires less time cleaning.

Durability
Integral metal anchor piece + integral tracking piece have less chance to be broken or partially lost.
